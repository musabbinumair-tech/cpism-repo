Punjab

Punjab is the second largest province of Pakistan by land area, and it is the most populated province, with an estimated 53% of the country's population. Punjab is known for its ancient cultural heritage, with many important archaeological sites, including the ruins of Taxila and Harrapa, which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
