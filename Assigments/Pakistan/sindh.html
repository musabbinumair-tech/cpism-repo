Sindh

Sindh is the second largest province of Pakistan by land area, with the largest population, mainly found near the Indus River. The province is home to two of the world's oldest cities, Mohenjo-Daro and Harappa, which date back to the Bronze Age. The province is also home to the Indus Valley Civilization, which is one of the oldest civilizations in the world.
