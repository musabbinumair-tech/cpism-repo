Balochistan

Balochistan is the largest province of Pakistan by land area, forming the southwestern region of the country. Its provincial capital and largest city is Quetta. Balochistan shares borders with Punjab and the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa to the northeast, Sindh to the east and southeast, and the Arabian Sea to the south. To the west, it shares borders with Iran, and to the northwest, it shares borders with Afghanistan.
